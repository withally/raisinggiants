Write the visual direction brief. This document is the creative spec for the landing page rebuild.

**CRITICAL — Use these inputs:**
1. The `design-system/MASTER.md` and `design-system/pages/landing.md` generated in Task 1
2. The 2026 trend research (`2026-TREND-RESEARCH-EXTRACTED.md`)
3. The frontend-design skill principles: bold aesthetic direction, distinctive, non-generic, "what makes this UNFORGETTABLE?"
4. The brand positioning from 06-03 (territory, emotional core, Kin name)
5. The current codebase state (what exists in components/landing/)

**CRITICAL — Apply frontend-design thinking:**
From the frontend-design skill: "Choose a clear conceptual direction and execute it with precision." The brief must commit to a BOLD direction, not hedge. Read the full frontend-design SKILL.md and internalize its philosophy:
- Typography: "distinctive choices that elevate the frontend's aesthetics; unexpected, characterful font choices"
- Color: "Dominant colors with sharp accents outperform timid, evenly-distributed palettes"
- Spatial: "Unexpected layouts. Asymmetry. Overlap. Diagonal flow. Grid-breaking elements."
- Backgrounds: "Create atmosphere and depth rather than defaulting to solid colors"
- NEVER: "generic AI-generated aesthetics like overused font families, cliched color schemes, predictable layouts"

**Document structure:**

## Part 1: Design System (from skill output)
- Palette: the chosen colors with hex codes and emotional rationale
- Typography: the chosen font pairing with weights and sizes
- Style: the chosen visual style and its key effects
- Texture/Craft: how raw authenticity manifests (grain, hand-drawn, organic shapes, etc.)

## Part 2: Design Philosophy
- How the 2026 trends map to specific design decisions
- The formula: "#5 + #6, seasoned with #9" — but now grounded in the skill's recommendations
- What makes this page UNFORGETTABLE (the frontend-design question)
- The emotional arc of the scroll

## Part 3: Section-by-Section Composition Specs
For EACH of the 8 sections:
1. **Layout** — specific composition (not "generous whitespace" — actual structure)
2. **Typography** — exact font/weight/size choices per element
3. **Color** — background, text, accents per section
4. **Craft elements** — texture, hand-drawn, organic details
5. **The ONE bold moment** — what stops the scroll here
6. **What changes** — explicit diff from the current component

The 8 sections: HeroSection, SocialProof, ArchetypePreview, KOLCredibility, KOLNarrative, FAQ, SecondaryHero, Footer

## Part 4: What This Is NOT
Explicit exclusions. What styles/approaches are rejected and why.

## Part 5: Page Flow Map
Emotional arc showing background shifts, density changes, and rhythm.

**The brief should be specific enough that Plan 06-05 can translate each section directly into component code.**
