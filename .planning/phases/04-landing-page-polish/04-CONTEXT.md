# Phase 4: Landing Page + Polish - Context

**Gathered:** 2026-02-25
**Status:** Ready for planning

<domain>
## Phase Boundary

Credibility-forward landing page that communicates the bigger mission — helping people become better parents — and converts visitors into the free Mirror quiz. The page must present the full product ladder, integrate KOL credibility signals, and be fully mobile-responsive. Sophia's credentials are NOT on the landing page (About Us only).

</domain>

<decisions>
## Implementation Decisions

### Tone & voice
- Warm and personal — speaks directly to the reader's experience growing up
- Empathetic, reflective — "you've always sensed this" energy
- Not clinical or data-forward on the surface; warmth leads, research backs it up

### Headline & value proposition
- The landing page frames the bigger mission: helping people become better parents
- Product 1 (The Mirror) is the free entry point, not the whole story
- The headline should speak to the larger aspiration, not just "see what your parents did"
- The quiz is positioned as the starting point of a journey, not the destination

### Product ladder presentation
- Show the full product ladder explicitly on the landing page: Mirror → Blueprint → Partner Match
- Mirror: Understand your own upbringing (free)
- Blueprint: Understand your own tendencies and aspirations (paid)
- Partner Match: Have a dialogue with your partner about parenting (premium)
- Visitors should see the complete vision so they understand where this goes

### Credibility strategy
- Sophia is NOT on the landing page — she belongs in About Us at most
- The real credibility comes from named KOL researchers and their ideas
- Top parenting experts and research attribution are the trust signals
- The expertise of the framework is what matters, not a single practitioner

### Claude's Discretion
- Visual tone, layout structure, above-the-fold design
- Section ordering and flow
- CTA placement, copy, and conversion approach
- How KOL credibility signals are displayed (cards, quotes, inline citations)
- Light vs dark theme, color palette, typography
- Animation and interaction design
- Mobile-specific layout adaptations

</decisions>

<specifics>
## Specific Ideas

- The three products map to a clear journey: (1) understand your upbringing, (2) understand your tendencies/aspirations, (3) have a dialogue with your partner
- Each product is distinct — the landing page should make this ladder feel like a natural progression
- Warmth and empathy over clinical authority in the copy

</specifics>
