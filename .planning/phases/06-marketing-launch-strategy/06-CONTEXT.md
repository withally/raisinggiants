## Implementation Decisions

### Content & Social Strategy
- Primary platforms: Instagram + TikTok — where parenting content thrives (Reels/TikToks for discovery)
- Content mix: alternate between relatable micro-content ("You parent like this because you were raised like that" emotional hooks) and educational research snippets (credibility-building KOL-backed insights)
- Tone testing: plan content across three tones — warm+vulnerable (personal stories, "you're not alone"), expert+approachable (Sophia's voice, clinical but human), bold+provocative (challenge conventional parenting wisdom). Test all three to find what resonates.
- Pre-launch buildup: 4-8 weeks of content before product goes live — build audience, tease the quiz, collect waitlist emails. Launch to a warm audience, not cold.

### Email Funnel Design
- Capture point: email collected on pre-quiz landing page as waitlist signup — before users enter the quiz
- Pre-launch nurture: 3-5 email sequence over 2-4 weeks (welcome, parenting insight, "why your upbringing matters", archetype teaser, launch announcement)
- Post-purchase flow: value-first reflection tips after PDF delivery ("how to use your guide", 1-week check-in with reflection prompt), then partner nudge after 3-5 days ("share with your partner" + couple's Blueprint v2 teaser). Builds goodwill before asking for sharing.
- Abandoned quiz / non-purchasers: not discussed — Claude's discretion on whether to include

### Claude's Discretion
- Email platform choice (Resend already in stack for transactional; may need a marketing email tool like ConvertKit/Kit for sequences — research and recommend)
- Posting cadence and content calendar structure
- Content pillar framework specifics
- Who "creates" the content (AI-assisted, Sophia-authored, etc.)
- Whether to include abandoned quiz re-engagement emails
- Specific KOL/influencer outreach tactics (not discussed — can be scoped during planning)
- Paid acquisition channel identification (not discussed — can be scoped during planning)
