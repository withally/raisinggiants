# Phase 5: Landing Page — Visual Direction (Locked)

**Decided:** 2026-03-01
**Status:** Approved by founder

## Summary

The landing page visual direction sits **between "Warm Solid" and "Mid Blend"** — approximately 35-40% on the spectrum from fully graphic/opaque pastels toward atmospheric/glassmorphic tech.

## What This Means Concretely

### Layout
- **Asymmetric split**: 7-col left hero + 5-col right stacked modules
- Conversion-optimized: pills (objection reduction) → headline → CTAs → proof cards
- Degrades naturally to mobile (left column stacks as: pills → headline → CTAs → then right-column cards below)

### Cards & Surfaces
- **Hero card**: Solid-to-soft butter tint — not fully opaque like pure Asymmetric, but NOT glassmorphic. A warm pastel fill that has just a hint of translucency
- **Quote card**: Solid pink fill, confident — closer to opaque than glass
- **Stat cards**: Dark teal (#002833) with pastel accent numbers (pink, mint)
- **Footer bar**: Dark (#1A1A1A)
- **Border radius**: 24px throughout (organic modularity)
- **Borders**: Minimal — pastel-colored borders only on pills, not on cards

### Background
- Warm base: #FAFAF7 (not pure white)
- Very subtle gradient wash at top — just a whisper of pink/butter, not full mesh blobs
- Background should feel warm but not call attention to itself

### Typography
- **Headings**: Plus Jakarta Sans 800 (bold) + Space Grotesk 700
- **Accent**: Instrument Serif italic for "see what" in the headline — solid plum (#4A1942), NOT gradient text
- **Body**: Plus Jakarta Sans 400-500
- **Mixed serif + sans in headline**: "Finally" (bold sans) / "see what" (italic serif in plum) / "shaped you." (bold sans)

### Color Palette (from founder's reference)
| Color | Light (pastel) | Dark |
|-------|---------------|------|
| Blue | #B3D5DE | #002833 |
| Pink | #EEC0DA | #4A1942 |
| Mint | #B2DECD | #1A4A3A |
| Butter | #FEF4AC | #3D3B1A |

### Pills / Tags
- Solid pastel fills (not semi-transparent)
- Matching dark text color per palette pair
- Round pill shape (border-radius: 100px)

### Buttons
- Primary CTA: Dark teal (#002833), white text, 16px radius — solid, no gradient
- Secondary: Transparent with subtle border

### Nav
- Glassmorphic pill nav (the one modern/tech touch)
- backdrop-filter: blur(12px), rgba white background
- Segmented toggle style (The Mirror / About)

### What NOT to Do
- No gradient text on headline — keep it solid plum
- No gradient borders on cards — save for a future premium feel if needed
- No heavy gradient mesh blobs — keep the background subtle
- No fully glassmorphic cards — the warmth comes from visible pastel color, not glass
- Cards should feel tangible, not floating

## Design References
- **Alto Roasters** (Behance) — bold typography, color confidence
- **Dia Browser** — warm gray base, glassmorphic nav, soft atmosphere
- **Lumerion** (Behance) — pastel + dark pairings, modern brand identity
- Founder's pastel palette image — the four light/dark pairs above

## Brand Context
- **Brand**: Kin
- **Product**: The Mirror (free quiz)
- **Territory**: "Digital tools for parents who want to understand themselves"
- **Emotional core**: "I always sensed this. Now I see it."
- **Register**: Warm, bold, modern — not clinical, not childish, not generic startup
